Challenge 6 — Stored XSS Intermediate

AirNova allows passengers to leave reviews for flights. Reviews are saved and shown to all visitors without sanitization. Unlike reflected XSS, stored XSS runs for every user who visits the page.

Leave a review — Flight AN 447

Your mission

Post a review containing a JavaScript payload. Try:

<img src=x onerror="alert('Stored XSS!')">

Step up: make the payload steal the session cookie: <img src=x onerror="alert(document.cookie)">

Key difference from reflected XSS: this payload persists and fires for every subsequent visitor — exactly how Magecart injected persistent payment skimmers.
